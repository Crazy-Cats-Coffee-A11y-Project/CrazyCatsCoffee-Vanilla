Events

Are you looking for something fun and social to do? At Crazy Cats Coffee we have regular events in our quite lounge space and patio.

Storytime

Every Tuesday and Thursday at 10:30am, we have storytime for children and their caregivers. We read a book and have a craft activity. Free coffee and tea for adult members. A free mini-cookie for all children (nut, dairy, and gluten-free avaiable).
